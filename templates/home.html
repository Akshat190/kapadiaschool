About- HBK

Sunrise Academy was founded in 2002, starting with a modest group of students eager to learn. Over the years, Sunrise Academy has grown into a vibrant institution, nurturing a diverse array of students who shine brightly both locally and internationally. Situated in the heart of the community, Sunrise Academy is a co-educational English medium school dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth in a supportive environment.

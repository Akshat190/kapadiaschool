About Our School

Excellence in Education Since 1956

Kapadia High School was founded in 1956, starting with a modest group of students eager to learn. Over the years, our institution has grown into a vibrant educational center, nurturing a diverse array of students who shine brightly both locally and internationally.

Situated in the heart of the community, Kapadia High School is a co-educational English medium school dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth in a supportive environment.

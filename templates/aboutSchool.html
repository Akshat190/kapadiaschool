H.B.Kapadia High School

Sunrise Academy, established in 2002, began its journey with a small yet passionate group of students and educators who shared a vision of academic excellence and holistic development. Over the years, this humble institution has blossomed into a renowned educational establishment, recognized for its commitment to nurturing young minds. The school, nestled in the heart of the community, has become a beacon of learning and personal growth. With a sprawling campus that houses state-of-the-art facilities, Sunrise Academy offers a dynamic and engaging environment where students are encouraged to explore their talents and pursue their ambitions.

The academy’s co-educational, English-medium curriculum is meticulously designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and leadership. Guided by a team of dedicated teachers, students at Sunrise Academy receive not only a robust academic education but also the values and skills needed to thrive in an ever-changing world.

The diverse student body, like a garden in full bloom, represents a multitude of cultures and perspectives, creating a rich tapestry of experiences that enriches the learning journey. Graduates of Sunrise Academy carry the school’s legacy with them, spreading their wings across the globe and contributing positively to society.As Sunrise Academy continues to grow and evolve, it remains steadfast in its mission to inspire and empower the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and innovators. The school is not just an institution; it is a nurturing community where every student is valued, every achievement is celebrated, and every dream is given the wings to soar.
